# ATTENDANCE ACCOUNTING METHOD RULES

## SEMESTER_FULL_TERM
METHOD: IGNORE_HOLIDAYS
DESCRIPTION: For full-term semester courses, scheduling calculations should ignore holidays. The total contact hours are divided by the standard number of class meetings for the term, regardless of holidays.

## SEMESTER_SHORT_TERM
METHOD: COUNT_HOLIDAYS
DESCRIPTION: For semester courses that are not full-term, scheduling calculations must account for holidays. Holidays are excluded from the count of total instructional days.

## INTERSESSION
METHOD: COUNT_HOLIDAYS
DESCRIPTION: For all intersession courses (Winter, Summer), scheduling calculations must account for holidays. Holidays are excluded from the count of total instructional days.
